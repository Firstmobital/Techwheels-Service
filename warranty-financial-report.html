Report 2 — 20% Parts Revenue (Dealer Margin)

💹
20% dealer margin on warranty parts — MRP × 20%
warranty_claim_settlement_report_data · list_price field = MRP · NDP = what TM pays back · Margin = list_price − NDP
Margin calculation logic
💡list_price in settlement register = MRP (manufacturer's retail price for the part).
💡ndp (Net Dealer Price) = amount TM actually pays back to dealer.
💡Dealer margin = list_price − ndp. TM policy: dealer earns 20% of MRP on warranty parts replacement.
📊Settlement register ALL lines: list_price sum = ₹1,47.31L, NDP sum = ₹1,26.47L, TM margin deduction = ₹20.84L. Dealer 20% margin = ₹29.46L from all posted lines.
